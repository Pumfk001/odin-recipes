Tofu Stir Fry

Stir fried noodles with chunks of crispy tofu

Packed with veg and plenty of flavour, this quick and easy meal is perfect in any season.
Easy adaptable for a variety of different veg, it's a great way to use up whatever you have in fridge!

Ingredients for 2 People

  • 1 block of firm tofu
  • 1 onion
  • 1/2 head broccoli
  • 1 red pepper
  • 150g baby corn
  • 150g frozen edamame
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tbsp gochjuang
  • 1 inch of ginger
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 portions of noodles

Steps

  1. Cut vegetables to preferred size
  2. Heat oil in a wok
  3. Add onion and fry for 5 minutes
  4. Add all the remaining veg and cook for 8 minutes
  5. Chop the tofu into bite sized pieces and add to wok
  6. To make the sauce mix the soy sauce, gochjuang, grated garlic and grated ginger together in a bowl
  7. Prepare the noodles per instructions
  8. Add the sauce to the wok and cook for 1 minute
  9. Serve and season with more soy sauce to taste
