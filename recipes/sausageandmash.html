Sausage and Mash

A delicious sweet potato alternative to the pub classic, sausage and mash

The perfect mid-week meal for when you're craving something hearty but healthy.
The sweet potato mash is smooth and sweet and works perfectly with vegetarian sausages.

Ingredients for 2 People

  • 1 large sweet potato
  • 1/2 head broccoli
  • 160g of petit pois
  • 6 vegetarian sausages
  • 2 tsp english mustard
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • salt
  • ketchup or gravy to serve

Steps

  1. Boil Water
  2. Chop potato into medium chunks
  3. Add potato to boiling water and cook for 15 minutes or until soft
  4. Fry sausages for 10 minutes
  5. Add peas and broccoli to seperate pan of boiling water for 6 mins
  6. Drain potatoes and add back to pan
  7. Mash the potato with butter and mustand
  8. Make gravy per instructions on packet gravy, if using.
  9. Season with salt to taste
