Buddha Bowl

Buddha bowl packed with veg, bulgur wheat and Linda McCartney duck

Easily adjustable for whatever veg/protein/carb you fancy, a buddha bowl is a great dish to have week in week out.
Feel free to adapt the recipe below however you please!

Ingredients for 2 People

  • 1 pack Linda McCartney duck substitute
  • 150g bulgur wheat
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 avocado
  • 1/3 cucumber
  • 1 red pepper
  • 150g frozen edamame
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 1/2 lime
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Vegetable Bouillon

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Add bulgur wheat to boiling water and cook for 20 minutes
  3. LOREM IPSUM
